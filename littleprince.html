CONSUELO DE
SAINT-EXUPÉRY


The salvadoran who inspired "The Little Prince"

BIOGRAPHY

Consuelo Suncín, comtesse de Saint Exupéry, was a Salvadoran writer and artist, and the wife of Antoine de Saint-Exupéry, a pioneering French aviator and author of "Le Petit Prince." She was his muse, the inspiration for The Little Prince's beloved rose.
